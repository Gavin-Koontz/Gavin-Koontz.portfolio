About Me

Hi, I'm Gavin Koontz. Growing up on a cattle and row crop farm, my love for technology began early. Known as the family's go-to "Tech Genius," I handled everything from computer issues to spreadsheet dilemmas. My first coding experience involved tinkering with Windows cmd, batch, and PowerShell. However, I found my true passion in HTML, creating small websites for fun. Alongside my tech adventures, I nurtured my creative side, frequently indulging in art. As I matured, I discovered that web development seamlessly blended coding and creativity, making it the perfect choice for me. Today, I'm pursuing a degree in web development, while also managing our family farm and nurturing my artistic side. Welcome to my portfolio, where I showcase my journey and creations.
