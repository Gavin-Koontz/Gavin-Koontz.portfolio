About Me

Hello, my name is Gavin Koontz. I grew up as a cattle and row crop farmer. At a young age, I began to develop an interest in technology. As my family noticed that I was a "Tech Genius," I was the one they called when they had tech problems, whether it be a simple restart of the computer or help with their spreadsheet - I was the go-to person.

My first 'coding' experience involved messing around with Windows cmd, batch, and PowerShell. I had a lot of fun with these, but my most enjoyable and well-understood experience was with HTML. I started creating random little websites all the time. Alongside my tech adventures, I was also very creative and frequently engaged in art creation.

As I grew older, I realized that web development involved a lot of coding and creativity - this is what I wanted to do. Today, I am pursuing a degree in web development while still managing my family farm and creating art on the side. Welcome to my portfolio.
