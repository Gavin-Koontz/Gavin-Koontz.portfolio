Magazine Mock Up

In my Photoshop class, I crafted a dynamic magazine cover by merging multiple images. I utilized diverse filters, fonts, and hand-painted shadows beneath a skateboarder. Creating this mock-up was an enjoyable exploration of design techniques, from image merging to typography, offering a platform to blend creativity with digital artistry.
