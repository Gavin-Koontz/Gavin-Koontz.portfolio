SportsXtreme Magazine Cover

In my Photoshop class, I created a cohesive magazine cover by merging multiple images. I used many filters, fonts, and techniques throughout my design. Specifically, I used hand-painted shadows beneath the skateboarder. The text placement and visual depth were most prominent in the title and were accomplished by using many layers. This mock-up creation was an enjoyable challenge of design techniques, from image merging to typography. This project blended my creativity with digital art.

Midnight Suns Album Cover

In 'Midnight Sun,' I used a nighttime shot and added a sun and Rock and Roll sign in the back. This imagery was added to mirror the name of the band and to highlight the genre of music. The background photos were added separately. I seamlessly merged the nighttime sky and mountain images while also adding texture to the mountain layer. I then added the album and band name text using a font that contributed to the theme of the design. I finished the design by adding a parental advisory sticker at the bottom right.
