SportsXtreme Magazine Cover

In my Photoshop class, I created a cohesive magazine cover by merging multiple images. I used many filters, fonts,and techniques throughout my design. Specifically I used hand painted shadows beneath the skateboarder. The text placement and visual depth was most prominent in the title and was accomplished by using many layers. This mock-up creation was an enjoyable challange of design techniques, from image merging to typography. This project blended my creativity with digital art.

Midnight Suns Album Cover

In "Midnight Sun" I used a nightime shot and added a sun and Rock and Roll sign in the back. This imagery was added to mirror the name of the band and to highlihgt the genre of music.
